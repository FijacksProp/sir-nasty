Staking

SN staking is implemented via an Anchor (Rust) smart contract on Solana. Users stake directly from their wallets using the dedicated staking dApp.

All staking operations—including deposits, rewards calculation, vault management, and claims—are handled fully on-chain, ensuring transparency and security.
